Starry Dome House


Starry Dome House, produced using imported PC (polycarbonate) panels from Germany with unique thermoform technology, is resistant to UV, wind, and precipitation. It can be used outdoors for 10 years without yellowing, breaking, or cracking, and offers a panoramic view of the starry sky. It requires no infrastructure, is in the portable structure class; suitable for temporary installations not subject to permits and does not harm the environment.





Starry Dome House


It is produced using PC (polycarbonate) panels with advanced thermoform technology. Thanks to its structure resistant to UV rays, wind, and precipitation, it can be safely used outdoors for many years without yellowing, breaking, or cracking. It offers the opportunity to view the starry sky panoramically. It requires no infrastructure, is a portable solution; suitable for temporary and environmentally friendly installations.

  • - Cafe, restaurant and lounge areas
  • - Event, exhibition and showroom uses
  • - Garden, terrace and winter garden solutions
